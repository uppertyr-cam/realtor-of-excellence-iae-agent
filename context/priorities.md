# Priorities

## Confirmed From Public Positioning

- Maintain a technology-led operating identity.
- Support buyers and sellers with a smoother, more confidence-building process.
- Present ROE as a modern alternative to traditional branch-heavy real estate models.
- Grow and sustain international credibility through the Swiss and offshore offering.
- Attract and retain agents through flexibility, training, and cloud-based infrastructure.

## Inferred Commercial Priorities

- Increase listing exposure and conversion through better marketing and faster lead handling.
- Improve buyer qualification and handoff speed.
- Use automation and CRM structure to reduce admin drag and missed follow-up.
- Strengthen trust signals that help convert both sellers and serious buyers.
- Continue building premium and international deal flow without losing South African operating credibility.

## Inferred Audience Priority Order

- Sellers and listings appear commercially important because official messaging emphasizes exposure, marketing quality, buyer reach, and seller-first positioning.
- Qualified buyers are likely high-value because the system already invests heavily in re-engagement and routing automation.
- Agent recruitment is a meaningful strategic lever given the prominence of the "Join our Family" proposition.
- International and offshore clients are a brand and margin lever, especially through ROE Swiss.

## Open Client-Confirmation Items

- Which service line matters most in the next 90 days: listings, buyer conversion, rentals, international, or recruitment.
- Which geographic areas deserve the highest automation and messaging priority.
- Whether recruitment growth is a current core priority or a secondary brand-support initiative.
- Which outcomes matter most commercially: speed to close, listing volume, conversion rate, revenue per agent, or admin reduction.

## Operational Implication

- Until client priorities are confirmed, automation should optimize for responsiveness, qualification quality, CRM cleanliness, and professional brand consistency.
