# Audiences

## Residential Buyers

- Care about trust, guidance, affordability, timing, and finding the right fit.
- ROE tends to emphasize support, clarity, and confidence through the buying process.
- Tone should be reassuring, practical, and patient.
- CTA style: book a viewing, discuss options, continue qualification, or speak with an agent.

## Sellers

- Care about exposure, pricing confidence, speed, professionalism, and security.
- ROE emphasizes marketing quality, buyer reach, local and international exposure, and seller-first cooperation.
- Tone should be commercially confident and outcomes-focused.
- CTA style: request valuation, discuss sale strategy, or list the property.

## Renters / Tenants

- Care about responsiveness, fit, and a smooth move-in process.
- ROE frames rentals as supportive and family-oriented rather than transactional only.
- Tone should be clear, helpful, and low-friction.
- CTA style: enquire, schedule a viewing, or confirm requirements.

## First-Time Buyers

- Care about explanation, reassurance, and confidence in a high-stakes decision.
- ROE copy naturally supports this segment because it frequently highlights guidance and stress reduction.
- Tone should be educational, calm, and confidence-building.
- CTA style: ask questions, start the process, or speak with an advisor.

## Investors

- Care about strategy, opportunity, market knowledge, and risk-aware guidance.
- ROE's Swiss and international materials position the business as a strategic partner for investment-oriented property decisions.
- Tone should be informed, composed, and commercially credible.
- CTA style: review opportunities, discuss strategy, or explore offshore options.

## International / Offshore Clients

- Care about trust, expertise, premium access, and cross-border confidence.
- ROE uses a more elevated, bespoke, and internationally polished tone here.
- Tone should be premium, steady, and advisory.
- CTA style: explore listings, discuss strategy, or connect with the Swiss/international team.

## Agent Recruits

- Care about commission structure, flexibility, tools, support, training, and independence.
- ROE recruitment material emphasizes freedom from office constraints, cloud-based infrastructure, training access, and modern tools.
- Tone should be motivating but still businesslike.
- CTA style: join the family, review the package, or speak with leadership.

## Internal Team / Operators

- Care about lead clarity, next steps, ownership, and professionalism.
- Internal automations should be direct, factual, and easy to act on.
- Tone should be concise and operational.
- CTA style: review, follow up, qualify, close, or update the CRM.
