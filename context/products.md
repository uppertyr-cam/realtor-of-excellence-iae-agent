# Products and Services

- Residential property sales
- Residential rentals
- Commercial property
- Industrial property
- Farms and smallholdings
- Vacant land
- New developments

## Advisory and Support Services

- Property valuations
- Bond financing support
- Legal transfer support
- Rental administration
- Property administration
- International and offshore property investment advisory, with Switzerland as a notable focus
- Real estate agent training programs

## Public Framing

- Residential sales are framed around guidance, confidence, and protecting family investments.
- Rentals are framed as practical help for families and tenants rather than as a commodity listing function.
- Commercial and industrial services are positioned as tailored, opportunity-led solutions.
- New developments and international property are framed as premium access and strategic opportunity.
- Supporting services are presented as part of a smoother end-to-end client journey, not as isolated bolt-ons.
