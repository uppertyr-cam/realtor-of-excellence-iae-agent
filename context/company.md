# Realtor of Excellence

## Identity

- Realtor of Excellence is a South African real estate business with an affiliated European arm, ROE Swiss.
- The business positions itself around the tagline: "Redefining real estate through innovation and integrity."
- The company was founded in 1976 by Paul Enderli as Realtors International and later rebranded to Realtor of Excellence.
- Realtor of Excellence is recognized as South Africa's first cloud-based real estate company.

## Footprint

- South Africa headquarters: Cape Town, with coverage across 18+ local areas.
- European headquarters: Zug, Switzerland.
- Broader network presence includes the UAE, UK, Montenegro, Thailand, Indonesia, and Austria.

## Scale

- 60+ agents across the wider network.
- 100+ residential listings.
- Approximately 350,000 contacts in the global client network.

## Values

- Integrity
- Innovation
- Genuine Care
- Trust
- Excellence
- Community Service

## Regulatory

- Registered with the PPRA (Property Practitioners Regulatory Authority).

## Operating Posture

- Public-facing positioning combines family-business trust with technology-led execution.
- The South African business presents itself as modern, cloud-based, flexible, and relationship-driven rather than old-school branch-heavy.
- The Swiss arm extends the same identity into a more premium, investment-oriented, international property frame.

## Tone Snapshot

- ROE writes in a polished, reassuring, benefit-led voice.
- Messaging consistently links technology to better service, stronger exposure, and smoother client outcomes rather than treating technology as the product itself.
- The public brand leans "people-first professional" more than "hard-sell luxury."

## Operating Note

- The IAE Agent and related UpperTyr automations are operational tools that support Realtor of Excellence workflows. They are not the core business itself.
